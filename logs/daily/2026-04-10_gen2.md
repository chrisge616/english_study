## ⚠️ Mistakes & Corrections

❌ Misunderstood "within the company" as the whole company level  
✅ "Within" = internal comparison (department vs department), NOT the whole collective  

❌ Confused "relative within the company" as supporting the company goal  
✅ It actually means internal competition, NOT collective success  

❌ Thought "drastic" means exaggeration  
✅ "Drastic" = extreme / big impact (not exaggeration)  

❌ Misinterpreted "essentially irrelevant" as needing an object  
✅ It can stand alone when context is clear  

---

## 🧠 Key Concepts

- **Within vs Collective**
  - Within → internal comparison (smaller scope)
  - Collective → whole system (bigger scope)

- **Relative vs Collective Success**
  - Relative → comparing against others internally
  - Collective → shared success of the whole group

- **Language Intensity**
  - "essentially irrelevant" > "not important"
  - stronger, more decisive tone

- **Stylistic English (e.g., "reality hard")**
  - "hard" = intensifier (serious / fully committed)
  - informal but impactful style

- **Incremental Thinking**
  - progress = gradual, layered, long-term
  - opposite of sudden or drastic change
