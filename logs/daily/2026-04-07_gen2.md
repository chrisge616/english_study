## 🔑 Vocabulary

### High Priority

- Word: advance
  - Meaning (simple English): move forward or improve
  - Example: I want to advance my running skills.

- Word: advancement
  - Meaning (simple English): progress or improvement
  - Example: Her career advancement was slow but steady.

- Word: scope
  - Meaning (simple English): the range or variety of something
  - Example: I want to expand the scope of my reading.

- Word: scale
  - Meaning (simple English): the size or level of something
  - Example: The project grew in scale.

- Word: imply
  - Meaning (simple English): to suggest something indirectly
  - Example: He implied I was late.

- Word: counterintuitive
  - Meaning (simple English): opposite of what you naturally expect
  - Example: The solution is counterintuitive.

- Word: obstacle
  - Meaning (simple English): something that blocks progress
  - Example: Lack of time is an obstacle.

- Word: hindrance
  - Meaning (simple English): something that slows progress
  - Example: Rain was a hindrance.

- Word: compulsion
  - Meaning (simple English): a strong inner urge
  - Example: I felt a compulsion to finish the task.

- Word: molecule
  - Meaning (simple English): the smallest unit of a substance
  - Example: Water is made of molecules.


### Medium Priority

- Word: devote
  - Meaning (simple English): to give time or energy fully
  - Example: I devote myself to training.

- Word: overlap
  - Meaning (simple English): to share some common parts
  - Example: My skills overlap with my interests.

- Word: rigorous
  - Meaning (simple English): very thorough, strict, or precise
  - Example: He follows a rigorous routine.

- Word: alleviate
  - Meaning (simple English): to make pain or a problem less severe
  - Example: This helps alleviate stress.

- Word: within
  - Meaning (simple English): inside a limit or range
  - Example: I’ll finish within an hour.

- Word: enlightened
  - Meaning (simple English): having gained a deeper understanding
  - Example: I felt enlightened after reading it.

- Word: double-edged sword
  - Meaning (simple English): something with both benefits and drawbacks
  - Example: Social media is a double-edged sword.

- Word: tenacious
  - Meaning (simple English): persistent and unwilling to give up
  - Example: You must be tenacious.

- Word: broadly
  - Meaning (simple English): in a general or wide way
  - Example: Apply this broadly in life.

- Word: absurd
  - Meaning (simple English): ridiculous or illogical
  - Example: That idea is absurd.

- Word: proportionate
  - Meaning (simple English): balanced and matching the situation
  - Example: Punishment should be proportionate.


### Low Priority

- Word: propagate
  - Meaning (simple English): to spread or extend
  - Example: Ideas propagate over time.

- Word: radically
  - Meaning (simple English): completely or fundamentally
  - Example: This is a radically new idea.

- Word: torture (figurative)
  - Meaning (simple English): to push yourself very hard in a figurative sense
  - Example: I tortured myself to finish.

- Word: sentient
  - Meaning (simple English): able to feel and perceive
  - Example: Humans are sentient beings.

- Word: hyperrational
  - Meaning (simple English): extremely logical, often with little emotion
  - Example: He is very hyperrational.

- Word: axiom
  - Meaning (simple English): a basic truth accepted without proof
  - Example: It is an accepted axiom.

- Word: innate
  - Meaning (simple English): natural or inborn
  - Example: Humans have innate abilities.

- Word: congestion
  - Meaning (simple English): overcrowding or blockage
  - Example: Traffic congestion is bad.

- Word: tenfold
  - Meaning (simple English): ten times as much
  - Example: Productivity increased tenfold.

- Word: diameter
  - Meaning (simple English): the longest line across a circle
  - Example: The diameter is 10 cm.

- Word: radius
  - Meaning (simple English): half of the diameter
  - Example: The radius is half the diameter.

- Word: ingest
  - Meaning (simple English): to take in food or information
  - Example: We ingest information daily.
